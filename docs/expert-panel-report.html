Expert Panel Report - Ethical Model

Executive Summary

This report compiles feedback from 6 industry experts evaluating the KSU Research platform concept. After an initial review raised concerns about a hidden IP model, the platform pivoted to a fully transparent, ethical approach that received unanimous support from the expert panel.

"Let's go above board and compete with the ones coming out." - Founder Decision
🔍
Transparent AI AI matching is a marketed feature, not hidden. Researchers see exactly how recommendations are made.
🔒
No IP Claims Researchers and universities retain ALL intellectual property. Platform takes zero ownership.
💰
Institution Pays $75K-$150K/year subscription. Free for all faculty and researchers.
📈
VC Fund Model Separate fund invests in spinouts. Takes equity in companies, not researcher IP.
