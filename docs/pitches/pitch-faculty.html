FAQ

Q: Is this free?
A: Yes, for all KSU faculty and researchers. The institution covers the cost.

Q: Do I have to use it?
A: No. Participation is optional. You can claim your profile, ignore it, or actively opt out.

Q: Can I hide certain projects?
A: Yes. You have full control over what's visible and what's included in AI matching.

Q: Who sees my contact info?
A: Only what you choose to share. You can enable/disable email, phone, and office visibility.

Q: Where does my data go?
A: Nowhere outside KSU. Everything runs on university servers. No cloud services.
