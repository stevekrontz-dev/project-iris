Strategic Initiative

Elevating KSU's Research Profile Through AI-Powered Collaboration

Prepared for the Office of the Provost

Provost [Name],

As you work to elevate KSU's research profile and national ranking, I wanted to bring to your attention a strategic initiative that directly supports these goals - with zero risk to the institution.

The Strategic Imperative

University rankings increasingly weight research output, citation impact, and interdisciplinary collaboration. KSU's faculty have the talent - but they lack the infrastructure to discover collaboration opportunities across the institution.

A professor in Biology doesn't know that a colleague in Computer Science is developing machine learning techniques that could transform her research. A new hire in Engineering struggles to find mentors and collaborators outside their immediate department.

These missed connections directly impact:

  • Research output and publication quality
  • Grant competitiveness (NSF/NIH increasingly favor cross-disciplinary teams)
  • Faculty satisfaction and retention
  • Time to productive collaboration for new hires

The Solution: KSU Research Platform

We've built an AI-powered platform called IRIS (Intelligent Research Information System) that:

  • Analyzes faculty profiles, publications, and research interests
  • Identifies non-obvious collaboration opportunities across colleges
  • Connects researchers who would benefit from working together
  • Accelerates new faculty integration into the research community
Key Differentiator: Unlike cloud-based tools from Clarivate or Elsevier, IRIS runs entirely on KSU infrastructure. No faculty data ever leaves the institution. This addresses export control concerns, protects pre-publication research, and builds the trust needed for faculty adoption.

Strategic Benefits

  • Research Rankings: Increased cross-disciplinary publications and citation impact
  • Grant Success: Better team composition leads to more competitive proposals
  • Faculty Recruitment: "We have AI tools to help you find collaborators" is a compelling pitch
  • Faculty Retention: Researchers who feel connected stay longer
  • Institutional Visibility: "KSU launches innovative AI research platform" generates positive press

Risk Mitigation

Zero Financial Risk: We're proposing a free 12-month pilot with select departments. If the platform doesn't demonstrate measurable impact (new collaborations, grant submissions, faculty satisfaction), KSU walks away with no cost or obligation.

Additional safeguards:

  • Full data privacy - no external cloud services
  • FERPA compliant architecture
  • Faculty opt-out capability
  • Transparent AI - researchers see exactly how matches are generated

Alignment with Strategic Plan

This initiative directly supports KSU's strategic priorities:

  • Expanding research and creative activity
  • Fostering innovation and interdisciplinary work
  • Supporting faculty success
  • Leveraging technology for institutional advancement

Recommendation

I recommend a brief meeting with your VP of Research and CIO to evaluate this opportunity. The pilot requires:

  • Selection of 2-3 pilot departments
  • IT coordination for on-premise deployment
  • Research Office support for faculty communication

I'm happy to provide a demonstration and answer any questions about the technical architecture, privacy safeguards, or implementation timeline.
