What KSU Research Does

IRIS (Intelligent Research Information System) is an AI that analyzes researcher profiles, publications, and grants to identify non-obvious collaboration opportunities.

  • Automated Discovery: IRIS finds potential collaborators faculty would never discover on their own - especially across colleges
  • Grant Matching: Real-time alerts when funding opportunities align with a researcher's profile
  • Co-PI Suggestions: When a faculty member starts a grant application, IRIS suggests potential co-investigators with complementary expertise
  • Equipment Utilization: Connect researchers with shared equipment across campus - reducing duplicate purchases

ROI You Can Measure

Projected Impact (Conservative Estimates):

  • +10% improvement in grant success rates through better team composition
  • $200K+ annual savings through equipment sharing
  • Measurable increase in cross-college co-authored publications

Why This Is Different

Full Data Privacy: Unlike Clarivate or other cloud solutions, IRIS runs entirely on KSU servers. No researcher data ever leaves your infrastructure. This matters for:

  • Export-controlled research
  • Pre-publication work (no risk of data leaks)
  • FERPA compliance
  • Faculty trust and adoption

Transparent AI: Faculty see exactly WHY IRIS suggests a match. No black box. This builds trust and encourages adoption.
